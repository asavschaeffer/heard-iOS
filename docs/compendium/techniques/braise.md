## Overview
Sear then cook slowly with a small amount of liquid in a covered vessel.

## Rules
Maintain a gentle simmer to avoid tightening proteins.

## When to Break
Reduce uncovered at the end to concentrate sauce.

## Notes
Ideal for tougher cuts and fibrous vegetables.
