## Overview
Quick cooking in a small amount of fat over relatively high heat.

## Rules
Keep ingredients in motion for even browning and prevent scorching.

## When to Break
Allow brief contact for browning when developing fond is desired.

## Notes
Best for small, uniform pieces to avoid steaming.
