## Flavor Profile
Pungent raw, sweet and savory when cooked.

## History
Foundational aromatic across cuisines worldwide.

## Rules
Sweat slowly for sweetness; high heat can scorch and turn bitter.

## When to Break
Char quickly for smoky sweetness in salsas and broths.

## Pairings
Works with `compendium://ingredient.tomato` and
`compendium://ingredient.garlic` as a base.

## Substitutions
Use a smaller amount of garlic for aroma-only swaps.

## Notes
Salt draws moisture and speeds softening.
