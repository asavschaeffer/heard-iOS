## Flavor Profile
Sweet and herbal with a mild peppery finish.

## History
Used across Mediterranean and Southeast Asian cuisines with regional variants.

## Rules
Add late to preserve volatile aromatics.

## When to Break
Infuse into oils or creams for a deeper aromatic base.

## Pairings
Pairs cleanly with `compendium://ingredient.tomato` and
`compendium://ingredient.olive-oil`.

## Substitutions
None that match exactly; use fewer leaves rather than over-substituting.

## Notes
Basil bruises easily; tear rather than chop for fresh applications.
