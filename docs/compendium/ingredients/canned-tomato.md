## Flavor Profile
Stewed, savory tomato flavor with stable acidity.

## History
Canning makes tomato flavor available year-round.

## Rules
Simmer gently to avoid metallic notes from long, high-heat cooking.

## When to Break
Use straight from the can in rustic sauces and stews.

## Pairings
Base aromatics like `compendium://ingredient.onion` and
`compendium://ingredient.garlic` are common.

## Substitutions
Fresh `compendium://ingredient.tomato` works when in season.

## Notes
Choose whole peeled for the cleanest flavor.
