## Flavor Profile
Fruity and grassy with a peppery finish, especially when extra virgin.

## History
Central to Mediterranean cooking for millennia.

## Rules
Use extra virgin for raw applications; use milder oil for high-heat frying.

## When to Break
Finish stews with a drizzle for aroma even after long cooking.

## Pairings
Classic with `compendium://ingredient.tomato`,
`compendium://ingredient.garlic`, and `compendium://ingredient.basil`.

## Substitutions
Neutral oils can replace it in cooking, but flavor will differ.

## Notes
Store away from light and heat to reduce oxidation.
