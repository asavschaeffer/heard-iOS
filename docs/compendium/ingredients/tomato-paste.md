## Flavor Profile
Deep, concentrated tomato flavor with assertive acidity.

## History
Developed to preserve tomato flavor and increase intensity in sauces.

## Rules
Bloom in fat briefly to remove raw metallic notes.

## When to Break
Stir in directly for quick body when time is tight.

## Pairings
Pairs well with `compendium://ingredient.garlic` and
`compendium://ingredient.olive-oil`.

## Substitutions
For longer cooking, use `compendium://ingredient.canned-tomato` instead.

## Notes
A little goes a long way; balance with fat and salt.
